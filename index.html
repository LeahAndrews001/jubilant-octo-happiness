Clean Water: A Precious Resource

Understanding the importance of clean and safe water for everyone

Importance Sources Conservation Facts

Why Clean Water Matters

Clean water is essential for life. Without it, humans, animals, and plants cannot survive. Access to clean water helps prevent diseases, improves health, and supports sustainable development.

Sources of Clean Water

  • Rivers and lakes
  • Groundwater from wells
  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Water treatment plants

Conservation Tips

  • Fix leaks immediately to avoid wastage
  • Use water-saving fixtures and appliances
  • Recycle and reuse water whenever possible
  • Practice responsible agriculture to prevent contamination

Did You Know?

  • Over 2 billion people live without safe drinking water.
  • Waterborne diseases cause more than 485,000 deaths annually.
  • About 70% of global freshwater is used for agriculture.
